About me

I am Ângelo Rafael da Silva Coelho, born in the Outomn of 1995 in the city of Braga. I am a Computer Science graduate (level 06) at University of Minho with interest in technologies in particular the information one.

At the moment I am in search of new challenges in the Software Development branch, being interested in acquiring knowledge and experience in a real industry scenario.

In the past years I've learnt a lot, professionally and academically speaking.

My student-worker status has gave me a good task and deadline management skills also providing me good adaptability to generic informatics tools and programming languages having resourcefulness with urging necessities.

My waiter and barman experience instructed me with great team work ability and oral communication in various natural languages, being totally comfortable with Portuguese (native) and English with moderate knowledge of French and Spanish.
